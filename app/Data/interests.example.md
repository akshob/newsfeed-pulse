Describe what you care about, in a paragraph or two. This file is the relevance rubric used by the `score` command — the LLM scores each item against this.

Example structure:

I'm a [profession/role], and I care about [top interests]. Deep technical content in those areas is welcome.

I also want this feed to help me with [context — e.g. "conversations with my non-technical partner" or "staying current at work"]. For that, surface [specific categories — politics, cultural moments, human drama, etc.]. For political stories I often have opinions but lack historical context, so explainers are valuable there.

Skip: [what you don't want — generic press releases, cycle-of-the-day noise, topics you already follow elsewhere, etc.]

---

Tips:
- Be specific. "AI advancements" is weaker than "LLM inference on local hardware, developer tooling, model/provider updates".
- State what to *skip* as explicitly as what to surface.
- Don't agonize over the first draft — nightly evolution (not yet built) will tune it based on your keep/skip votes.
